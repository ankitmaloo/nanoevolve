## Why This Matters
The point is not only to remember winners.
The point is to remember why losers lost and what that implies about the space.

## Minimum Quality Bar
Do not close a loop log entry unless it states:
- one concrete thing learned about the search space,
- one hypothesis that got weaker,
- one hypothesis that got stronger,
- one thing the next loop must avoid repeating.
